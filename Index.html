Rules

Qualification Criteria:

  • Have completed at least 2 laser run events prior to this championship.
  • Be fully affiliated to SAMPA and the Province.
  • Register before the deadline of 10 July 2025.
  • Pay the full registration fee of R150.

Meeting these criteria is essential for participation in the championships.

General Laser Run Rules:

The competition will adhere to the latest UIPM (Union Internationale de Pentathlon Moderne) Laser Run rules and regulations. Key aspects include:

  • Shooting: Competitors must hit a target 5 times (or shoot for 50 seconds, whichever comes first) from a distance of 10 meters before proceeding to the run.
  • Running: After each shooting series, participants will complete a designated running distance. The number of shooting series and running distances vary by age category.
  • Equipment: Only approved laser pistols are permitted.
  • Fair Play: All participants are expected to demonstrate sportsmanship and adherence to rules.

A detailed rulebook can be requested via email or will be available at the event briefing.

Important Race Rules:

  • Lap Rule: The "lap rule" will be strictly enforced. Any runner who is lapped by the leading competitor in their heat will be eliminated from the race. This is for safety and to maintain race flow.
  • Age Categories and Distances: Specific distances for running and number of shooting series will be confirmed based on UIPM guidelines closer to the event. Typically:
  • Youth Categories: Shorter distances and fewer shooting series.
  • Senior Categories: Longer distances (e.g., 5x 600m run with 5 shooting series).

Detailed category information will be published on the registration form.
